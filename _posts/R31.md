<h1 id="cfa-level-1---corporate-finance---reading-31">CFA LEVEL 1 - Corporate Finance - Reading 31</h1>
<h2 id="introduction-to-corporate-governance-and-other-esg-consideration">Introduction to Corporate Governance and Other ESG Consideration</h2>
<p>Corporate Governance 公司的组织架构</p>
<blockquote>
<p>The  system  of  <em><strong>internal  controls</strong></em> and  procedures  by  which  individual  companies  are  managed.</p>
</blockquote>
<p><em><strong>遵循原则</strong></em><br>
（过去）Shareholder Theory<br>
（现在）Stakeholder Theory</p>
<p>Stakeholder：</p>
<ul>
<li>股东</li>
<li>其他利益相关者 (managers, Board of Directors, Creditors债权人, Suppliers, customers, Government, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<blockquote>
<p>Creditors  are  usually  not  entitled  to  any additional  cash  flows  (beyond  interest  and  debt  repayment)  if  the company’s  value  increases.</p>
</blockquote>
<h2 id="principal-agent-and-relationships-利益冲突">Principal-agent and Relationships 利益冲突</h2>
<p>股东（Principal） V.S. 高管（Agent）<br>
A  principal  hires  an  agent  to  perform  on  behalf  of  the  principal</p>
<blockquote>
<p>Conflict: Managers  may  seek  to  maximize  their  personal  benefits  to  the detriment（伤害）  of  shareholders’  interests.</p>
</blockquote>
<p><em><strong>三类利益冲突</strong></em></p>
<ol>
<li>Shareholders vs Managers
<ol>
<li>利益相关者关注长期目标，管理者关注短期效益</li>
<li>股东更愿意承担风险 risk tolerance, 而管理者 risk averse</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>大股东 (controlling) vs 小股东 (minority)<br>
投票权冲突<br>
1) straight voting (法定投票权) 一人一票， 不可积累<br>
2) Cumulative voting (累积投票权) 小股东可以通过抱团，影响决策<br>
双股权结构 multiple-class structure  <span class="katex--inline"><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math><semantics><mrow><mo>→</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\rightarrow</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.36687em; vertical-align: 0em;"></span><span class="mrel">→</span></span></span></span></span>  保护大股东<br>
X 股 1股1票投票权<br>
Y股  1股多票投票权</li>
<li>Shareholders vs Creditors<br>
风险承受力的冲突 （股东希望大风险大收益）</li>
</ol>
<h2 id="stakeholder-management">Stakeholder Management</h2>
<p>*** 冲突解决机制（4个）***</p>
<ul>
<li>Legal infrastructure 法律法规（外部）
<ul>
<li>defines rights established by law</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Contractual infrastructure 公司内部合同解决<br>
e.g. 跟manger签订长期合同。</li>
<li>Organizational infrastructure 公司组织架构变更<br>
e.g. 承诺manager未来 有进入董事会，成为大股东可能性</li>
<li>Governmental infrastructure 政府规范 （外部）
<ul>
<li>regulations  imposed on  companies</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><em><strong>Mechanisms  of  stakeholder  management</strong></em></p>
<p>General  meetings</p>
<ul>
<li>Annual  general  meeting  (AGM)  —  simple  majority （半数通过）
<ul>
<li>普通年度股东大会</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Extraordinary  general  meeting  (EGM)  —  supermajority  vote （e.g. 2/3通过）
<ul>
<li>临时股东大会，关于公司前途突发，重大决议</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="board-of-directors-and-committees-董事会（监管权）">Board of Directors and Committees 董事会（监管权）</h2>
<ol>
<li>Board members 董事
<ul>
<li>Executive 执行董事 （除了监管，还参与运营）</li>
<li>Independent 独立董事 （不参与运营）</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>One tier vs two tier
<ul>
<li>one tier: 执行董事，独立董事在一起开会</li>
<li>two tier: Separation  of  CEO  and  chairman</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Staggered board 分层董事会<br>
e.g. 董事会每次环节选举撤换并仅能撤换部分（防止恶意收购）</li>
</ol>
<p><em><strong>董事会职责</strong></em> Board of Director<br>
最主要的：</p>
<ul>
<li>领导及监管</li>
<li>指定组织架构</li>
<li>保证公司审计及内控系统</li>
<li>制动道德标准</li>
<li>指定风控系统 risk management  system</li>
</ul>
<p><em><strong>董事会下设委员会</strong></em> Committees (三大专业委员会)</p>
<ol>
<li>Audit committee 审计委员会</li>
<li>Remuneration/Compensation committee 薪酬委员会</li>
<li>Nomination committee 提名委员会
<ul>
<li>选人进董事会</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<blockquote>
<p>三大委员会成员必须是独立董事</p>
</blockquote>
<p>其他委员会 (负责日常运营，包括执行董事)</p>
<ul>
<li>Governance committee</li>
<li>Risk committee</li>
<li>Investment committee</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="factors-affecting-stakeholder-relationship">Factors Affecting Stakeholder Relationship</h2>
<ul>
<li>shareholder engagement</li>
</ul>
<blockquote>
<p>Involves annual shareholder meeting and analyst calls, the scope of which was limited to financial and strategic matters.</p>
</blockquote>
<ul>
<li>shareholder activism 股东激进主义</li>
</ul>
<blockquote>
<p>外部股东（小股东）积极参与公司重大经营决策以最大化利益的行为。（对冲基金常用策略）</p>
</blockquote>

<p><em><strong>ESG 选定方法</strong></em></p>
<ol>
<li>Negative screening 负面筛选清单
<ul>
<li>列出所有不符合条件的排除，在剩下的里面选</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Positive screening
<ul>
<li>列出所有符合的清单，在里面选</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Full integration
<ul>
<li>除了基本面，还要看ESG因素</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Thematic investing
<ul>
<li>话题投资，炒话题，ESG属于话题一种</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Engagement/active ownership investing
<ul>
<li>通过大量持股掌握经营权，进而改变公司运营策略，以改变ESG</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Green Finance 绿色融资
<ul>
<li>公司发债，融到的钱投入到ESG相关产业当中</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Overlap/Portfolio
<ul>
<li>投资时要综合考虑ESG factors</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
